Pretty Aesthetic Girl

  • Usually the class muse, multiple boys have a crush on her
  • Most of the time has no set priorites yet
  • Sticks to friends! Isn't two-faced unlike what others would think/say

Cute Aesthetic Boy

  • Usually the class escort, most girls look up to him
  • Isn't romantically focused most of the time
  • Typically a shy type, or if not, a very active student

National Book Store

  • Usually the supplier of pad papers, extra ballpens, etc. Always has a spare with them.
  • Please return their ballpens/pencils to them-
  • "Who has an ext-" "I do!" "Thank you!"

Athletic One™

  • Is in atleast one sports club, the sport they are passionate about!
  • "Time mo sa 1-mile run?" "7:25." "WHAT?!?"
  • Typical Jock, can be friendly, can be a jerk. Depends on who they talk to.

Make some noise!

  • Usually seated at the front/back
  • Conversation starters include "Alam mo ba crush ni-" and "Uy, uy, alam niyo ba kung ano nagyari kay-"
  • Rarely ever scolded, teachers are used to them

Literature is life~

  • Either a journalist/School newspaper writer before... May or may not write for Dalumat
  • Most of the time, they have a clipboard with them
  • Competitions regarding essay writing? A contestant, definitely.

Future Scientists

  • May be making puns regarding Physics, Chem, or Biology
  • Very Quick to derive Physics formulas, if not to figure out the Electron configuration of a certain element
  • Academics first- plus they'd prefer to be with their friends

Human Calculators

  • Can solve the problems on the board as soon as the teacher finishes writing them
  • Around 1.00 to 1.50 in terms of grades
  • Always participating contests, nationally or even internationally

Dance Dance! Revolution!

  • Usually the choreographer if the class when it comes to school everts like paskorus/sayaw ng dekada
  • Most probably a member of sagala, or if not, they did audition
  • You then see them practicing during class at times

Sugar Daddy™

  • The rich one in class, may tend to 'utang' from others still
  • Always the one to pay for class bonding time/food during parties
  • Gifts things to people at random, and all of a sudden. Very unpredictable

Creative Comittee

  • The artist in the class, the creative one who is asked to design/make layouts in projects
  • Is either doing Inktober 2019, cramming school requiremente, or both
  • Usually has a special medium where they specialize in (digital, pen, etc.)

Pagod™

  • Usually gets less than three hours of sleep, or more than 8 hours (impossible as a student of pisay -_-)
  • Tends to have coffee/anything with caffeine on them, still ends up asleep in at least class when they get bored
  • Zzzz zzzz zzzz zzzz

Natural Leader

  • While they tend to be the class president, it is always not the case
  • The one that unites the class when it comes to class projects, assigning the proper roles to everyone and does it well
  • Has a very approachable aura, or the opposite of it

Photo Person

  • Photographer of the class, the one always asked to take pictures
  • Do they have a camera? Yes. They mostly use their phone however because it's easier to edit.
  • WIll take pretty pictures of you when asked; just asks for credits when you post it

Singing Royalty

  • Sings almost every freetime. You can just suddenly hear someone in the hallway singing lyrics- the resident singer.
  • Low notes or high notes- no in between, lucky are those who can hit both.
  • Music grade? It's an obvious 1.00. And also? They are most probably a Himig member.

Public Speakers

  • No shame, and is great at oral presentations.
  • May or may be not be a debater, it depends on the person, honestly. Invictus club? Either active or too busy for it.
  • May not be a serious person most of the time, but is passionate about presentations.

Teacher's Pet

  • Can be annoying, and they are that person that is "sipsip/pabibo", usually just for a good grade
  • "Ma'am/Sir how may I help you?" or "Ma'am/Sir diba ako favorite student mo?"
  • Sits at the front/second row. Definitely.

mARUPOK™-

  • Gets crushes very easily- or is too smitten on that one certain crush
  • "Uy alam mo ba kanina, ang cute niya sa sout niyang jacket tas-"
  • Mostprobably tweets about their crush(es), at times- alam na nung crush niya.

Leader's P.A.

  • The leader's best friend, and the one they trust to help them.
  • Is also actually responsible! May be doubted at times, but they do their job well.
  • Substitute leader when the leader is busy/sick/can't handle the situation.

Trust in me~

  • Most, if not everyone in the class trusts them.
  • Probably knows at least half of the crushes of their classmates, and even the M.U.s
  • Very approachable, so it's easy to let out something to them. They're also not a gossip spreader, which is good.
